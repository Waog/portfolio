General information

We process personal data only to the extent necessary to provide this website, to communicate with you, or to pursue our legitimate interests.

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, in particular in the Member State of your habitual residence, your place of work, or the place of the alleged infringement.

Provision of the website

When you access this website, technically necessary data is processed (e.g., IP address, time of access, requested page, and browser/device information) to deliver, stabilize, and secure the website and to detect misuse.

The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in secure and reliable website operation).

For technical delivery we use, in particular, Cloudflare (DNS/CDN/network) and GitHub Pages (hosting). This may involve transfers to third countries (especially the United States); see the section “Transfers to third countries”.

Retention: log data is stored only as long as necessary for the stated purposes; exact retention periods can vary depending on provider and account settings.

Web analytics (Cloudflare Web Analytics / RUM)

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to statistically analyze website usage and improve performance. According to the provider, Cloudflare Web Analytics is designed to be cookie-free. Enabled Real User Measurements (RUM) are excluded for visitors from the EU; according to the Cloudflare configuration, the related JavaScript is not injected for visitors from the EU.

The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in reach measurement and performance optimization). Where the JavaScript-based measurement is active, it therefore applies only to non-EU visitors.

Contact

If you contact us by email, we process your contact details and the content of your message to handle your request. Emails are forwarded via Cloudflare Email Routing and processed in a Gmail mailbox; SMTP2GO may be used for sending emails.

The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (steps prior to entering into a contract / contract performance) and, where applicable, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in efficient communication).

If you contact us by phone, we process your phone number and communication content to handle your request. Telecommunications are carried out via the relevant telecommunications provider (e.g., Blau/Telefónica).

Transfers to third countries

Where processing by service providers takes place outside the EU/EEA (in particular in the United States), this is based on appropriate safeguards, in particular EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and/or certification under the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework (DPF), as stated by the respective provider.

Your rights

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)

To exercise your rights, you can contact us at info@oliverstadie.com.
